SOFTWARE REQUIREMENTS SPECIFICATION (SRS) FOR RT EXPRESS CARGO MANAGEMENT SYSTEM

1. INTRODUCTION

1.1 Purpose
This Software Requirements Specification (SRS) outlines the functional and nonfunctional requirements for a customer interaction platform for RT Express Co. Ltd., a company specializing in freight forwarding and logistics services. The platform aims to enhance customer experience by providing features such as cargo tracking, SMS updates, invoicing, and delivery status notifications.

1.2 Scope
The platform will serve as a comprehensive solution for RT Express customers to interact seamlessly with the company. Key functionalities will include real-time cargo tracking, automated SMS notifications, invoicing capabilities, and delivery status updates. The platform will be accessible via web and mobile interfaces.
